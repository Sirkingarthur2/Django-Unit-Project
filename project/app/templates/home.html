Introducing

  • Procurement Team

The procurement team at Basecamp Coding Academy is responsible for managing budgets to provide meals and essential supplies, such as soap and toilet paper. Our duties include researching and comparing prices from various suppliers and planning cost-effective menus. Occasionally, we also prepare the meals ourselves. In addition to meal planning, we are tasked with procuring various necessary products, like hand soap and T.P. but not limited to hygiene items.

Website Intro

     Welcome to my website, designed to streamline everything procurement. On the Home section, you'll find a guide on how to navigate and utilize the site. This section provides an overview of all the main areas, ensuring that users can easily find the information they need and understand how to interact with the different features available.

Blog Posts

     In the Blog section, Procurement posts updates and insights about our procurement activities. This area serves as a repository for all procurement-related news and other relevant information. It's an excellent resource for staying informed about the behind-the-scenes work.

Recipe Viewer

     The Recipe section is a dedicated space where procurement stores and shares the team's recipes. Here, you'll find a variety of our meal ideas that have been tried by our team. These recipes are designed to be cost-effective and easy.

Recommend a Recipe

     Finally, the Recommend Recipe area allows non-admin users to contribute their meal ideas. If you have a recipe that you think would be a great addition to our collection, this is the place to share it. By encouraging contributions from the school it ensures that we know what everyone likes.
